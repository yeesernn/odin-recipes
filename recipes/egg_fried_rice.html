Egg fried rice

Description

A very easy and delicious dish that Uncle Roger is obsessed with. (If you don't know him, look him up on YouTube)

The trick here is to use day old rice, and a extremely hot wok (or cast iron skillet).

Ingredients

  • Cooked long grain rice, day old is best
  • Oil
  • Salt and MSG
  • Eggs
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Chicken/Shrimp/Tofu (optional)
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Prepare the ingredients: crack the eggs into a bowl, diced up the veggies etc
  2. Add oil into your wok over high heat, wait until some smoke started coming out
  3. Crack the eggs and immediately stir them to break them up
  4. If you have chicken/shrimp/tofu, toss them in and stir for a minute
  5. Add the veggies and continue stirring for a minute
  6. Toss the rice in and break them up to prevent any clump. Stir for a few minutes
  7. Add a pinch of salt and MSG
  8. Dribble a dash of soy sauce and stir continuously to cover the grains evenly

There you have it, serve while its piping hot!
